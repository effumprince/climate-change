Causes of Climate Change

What Are Some of The Cause of Climate Change in Ghana?

In Ghana Climate Change is primarily caused by human activities
that increases concentrations of greenhouse gases
in the atmosphere, enhancing the natural greenhouse effects. While
natural factors play a role, the current rapid warming is unprecendented
and directly linked to industralizations.

Some Actions That causes Climate change in Ghana:

DEFORESTATION

In Ghana, deforestation is a significants
issue, with forests being cleared for timber
fuelwood, and agricultural expansion
(especially cocoa, oil palm, and rubber).
